Search Syntax

The following example uses all of the currently supported query syntax features:

"shareholder rights plan" +adopt -repeal

"shareholder rights plan" is referred to as a 'phrase query' and matches all terms within the quotes exactly.

+adopt makes the term adopt mandatory within the results.

You can make "shareholder rights plan" mandatory too with +"shareholder rights plan".

On the other hand, -repeal makes the term repeal prohibited.


Additional syntax features can be found in Section 4 of the "Overview of WRDS SEC Analytics Suite" document.
